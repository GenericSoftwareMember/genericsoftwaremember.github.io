The Chariot

The Chariot, the seventh card in the Major Arcana, represents determination, control, and triumph over challenges. Although the titular chariot is always present, the animals pulling it change between designs. It is sometimes shown as sphinxes, horses, or other animals, typically with a black-and-white motif.


Upright

When The Chariot appears upright in a reading, it signifies a period of victory, self-discipline, and control over conflicting forces. This card represents your ability to harness your inner strength and drive to overcome obstacles and achieve your goals. It encourages you to stay focused and determined, no matter the challenges you face. The Chariot suggests that you're on a path of accomplishment and personal growth, symbolizing a victorious journey ahead.


Reversed

In a reversed position, The Chariot may indicate a lack of direction or inner turmoil. You might be feeling overwhelmed or struggling to control conflicting aspects of your life. This card encourages you to regain focus and address any inner conflicts before proceeding. It can also signify delays or setbacks in your journey. Take time to reevaluate your goals and regain control over your path, ensuring that you're aligned with your true desires and values.
