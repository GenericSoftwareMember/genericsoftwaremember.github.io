The Priestess

Also known as the High Priestess, the Priestess is the second card that appears in the Tarot deck. She embodies mystery, intuition, and the subconscious. It has a great focus on believing in the inner wisdom within people.


Upright

When The High Priestess appears upright in a reading, it indicates a time of deep intuition and heightened awareness. Trust your instincts and tap into your inner wisdom. Secrets and mysteries may be revealed, and you are encouraged to explore your subconscious. This card suggests a need for patience and introspection before taking action. Listen to your inner voice and pay attention to dreams and intuitive insights.


Reversed

In a reversed position, The High Priestess can signal a lack of intuition or a reluctance to acknowledge your inner wisdom. You might be ignoring important insights or resisting your intuitive side. It could also point to hidden agendas or unresolved mysteries. This card urges you to reconnect with your intuition, explore your subconscious, and be open to the unknown. Avoid ignoring your inner guidance, as it may hold the key to understanding your current situation.
