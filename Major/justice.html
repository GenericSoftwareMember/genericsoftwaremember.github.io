The Justice

There can be some confusion between Justice and Strength, as the two swap numbers. For the purposes of this, the Justice shall be considered the eighth card. No matter the number, the concept of Justice does not change, embodynig fairness, balance, and moral judgment.


Upright

When Justice appears upright in a reading, it signifies fairness, truth, and impartiality. This card represents a need for rationality and ethical decision-making. It suggests that your actions and choices should be guided by principles of justice and integrity. Justice encourages you to seek truth, make balanced decisions, and take responsibility for your actions. It can also indicate that a legal or ethical matter will be resolved in your favor.


Reversed

In a reversed position, Justice may indicate unfairness, dishonesty, or a lack of accountability. You might be facing a situation where truth is hidden or justice is not served. This card encourages you to reevaluate your actions and decisions for any ethical lapses. It can also warn against making hasty or biased judgments. Take time to seek the truth, rectify any imbalances, and ensure your decisions align with your moral principles.
