The Death

Contrary to what one would assume from its name, Death in tarot is not actually a purely negative card. The thirteenth card is associated with major upheavals, transformation, renewal, and well, death.


Upright

When Death appears upright in a reading, it signifies a profound transformation or change that is inevitable and necessary for growth. This card represents the end of one phase of life, making way for a new and improved one. It encourages you to embrace the process of letting go, shedding old beliefs, and releasing attachments. While it can be unsettling, Death heralds a positive transformation, much like the changing seasons.


Reversed

In a reversed position, Death may indicate resistance to change or a fear of letting go. You might be holding onto the past or avoiding necessary endings. This card urges you to confront these fears and embrace change willingly. It can also suggest a stagnant situation that requires a shake-up to move forward. Avoiding change may lead to stagnation, so be open to the transformational process.
