The Judgment

Judgment, the twentieth card in the Major Arcana, represents rebirth, self-reflection, and spiritual awakening. It often features figures rising from coffins, symbolizing a call to a higher purpose. In the Thoth deck, it is replaced by the Aeon.


Upright

When Judgment appears upright in a reading, it signifies a period of self-reflection, renewal, and transformation. This card represents a call to awaken to your true self and embrace a higher purpose. It encourages you to let go of past mistakes, judgments, and regrets and to make amends or seek forgiveness if necessary. Judgment marks a moment of personal growth and a fresh start. It's a reminder to listen to your inner calling and live in alignment with your values and beliefs.


Reversed

In a reversed position, Judgment may indicate a reluctance to change, a fear of judgment, or resistance to self-reflection. You might be avoiding facing the consequences of your actions or neglecting your inner calling. This card encourages you to confront your past and make necessary changes for personal growth. It may also warn against procrastination or avoiding important decisions. Embrace the opportunity for transformation and self-awareness that Judgment offers, even if it feels uncomfortable.
