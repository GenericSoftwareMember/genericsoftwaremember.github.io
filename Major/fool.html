The Fool

The Fool is probably the most unique card within all the Major Arcana. It is either unnumbered or given 0 or XXII, meaning it can be pleased as first or last within the Fool's Journey. The fool in question is often thought to be a beggar, or someone of low social and economic standing.


Upright

The Fool in an upright position represents new beginnings, spontaneity, and embracing the unknown. It signifies a willingness to take risks, trust your instincts, and embark on a new journey with an open heart. This card encourages you to let go of the past and embrace uncertainty as you step into uncharted territory. It's a reminder that sometimes, we must take a leap of faith to discover our true path.


Reversed

When The Fool appears in a reversed position, it may indicate recklessness, impulsivity, or a reluctance to embrace change. You might be hesitating to take necessary risks or are holding onto past fears and inhibitions. It's a warning to balance your sense of adventure with practicality and caution, as impulsive decisions may lead to regret. Use this moment of reflection to reassess your goals and approach new opportunities with a bit more thoughtfulness.
