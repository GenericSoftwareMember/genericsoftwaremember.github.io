The Fortune

The Fortune, its full name actually being the Wheel of Fortune, symbolizes cycles, destiny, and change. It often features a large wheel with different creatures or figures representing the ever-turning wheel of fate. Notably, on this design of the Rider-Waite deck, it actually has one of the names associated with the Biblical God spelled out in Hebrew, YHWH.


Upright

When The Wheel of Fortune appears upright in a reading, it signifies a positive turn of events, luck, and the cyclical nature of life. This card represents a period of change, often for the better. It encourages you to embrace the ups and downs of life, knowing that everything moves in cycles. This is a reminder that good fortune and opportunities can unexpectedly come your way. Be open to change and go with the flow, as the wheel of life continues to turn.


Reversed

In a reversed position, The Wheel of Fortune may indicate a temporary setback, delays, or missed opportunities. You might feel like you're stuck in a negative cycle or facing obstacles. This card encourages you to exercise caution, especially in financial matters, and prepare for potential challenges. It also reminds you that, even in difficult times, the wheel will eventually turn in your favor. Focus on maintaining a positive outlook and adapt to changes as they come.
