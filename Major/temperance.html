The Temperance

Temperance, the fourteenth card in the Major Arcana, embodies balance, harmony, and moderation. It often features an angelic figure pouring liquid between two cups, symbolizing the blending of opposites.


Upright

When Temperance appears upright in a reading, it signifies a need for balance, patience, and moderation in your life. This card represents harmony and the ability to blend conflicting elements. It encourages you to find middle ground and approach situations with a calm and balanced demeanor. Temperance suggests that by avoiding extremes and embracing compromise, you can achieve inner and outer harmony.


Reversed

In a reversed position, Temperance may indicate a lack of balance, impatience, or excessive behavior. You might be struggling to find equilibrium in your life or be prone to impulsiveness. This card encourages you to reevaluate your actions and find ways to bring balance back into your life. It may also warn against overindulgence or hasty decisions. Take time to reflect and restore equilibrium in your thoughts, emotions, and actions.
