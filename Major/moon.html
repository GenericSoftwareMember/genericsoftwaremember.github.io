The Moon

The Moon, the eighteenth card in the Major Arcana, symbolizes intuition, illusion, and the subconscious mind. It often features a moonlit landscape with a crayfish emerging from water and two canines howling at the moon.


Upright

When The Moon appears upright in a reading, it signifies a period of heightened intuition, emotions, and mystery. This card represents the need to trust your instincts and delve deeper into your subconscious. It can suggest hidden truths, psychic experiences, or dream exploration. The Moon encourages you to embrace the unknown and face your fears with courage, knowing that not everything is as it seems. It's a reminder to rely on your inner guidance and let go of illusions.


Reversed

In a reversed position, The Moon may indicate confusion, deception, or a lack of clarity. You might be feeling disoriented or misled by your emotions. This card encourages you to seek clarity and avoid making hasty decisions. It may also suggest that you are not in touch with your intuition or are ignoring your inner wisdom. Take time for introspection and inner reflection to uncover hidden truths and navigate through the fog of uncertainty.
