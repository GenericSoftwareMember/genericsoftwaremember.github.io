The Star

The Star, the seventeenth card in the Major Arcana, symbolizes hope, inspiration, and spiritual guidance. It often features a woman kneeling beside a pool of water, pouring water from two jugs into a pond and gazing at a star-filled sky.


Upright

When The Star appears upright in a reading, it signifies a time of inspiration, hope, and healing. This card represents a renewed sense of purpose and a connection to higher guidance. It encourages you to have faith in the future and follow your dreams. The Star suggests that even in challenging times, there is a guiding light that can lead you toward greater fulfillment and happiness. Trust in your own intuition and the universe's plan for you.


Reversed

In a reversed position, The Star may indicate a lack of hope, doubt, or disconnection from your inner guidance. You might be feeling lost or struggling to find inspiration. This card encourages you to reconnect with your dreams and aspirations. It may also suggest that you need to address any feelings of hopelessness or negativity. Remember that even in difficult times, there is still a spark of hope and inspiration within you.
