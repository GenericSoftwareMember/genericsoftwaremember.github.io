The Lovers

The Lovers is the sixth card in the Major set and true to its name, is typically comprised of two people on the card. The Lovers represent love, partnership, and choices.


Upright

When The Lovers appear upright in a reading, it signifies a significant choice related to love, relationships, or partnerships. It represents harmony and mutual attraction, suggesting a strong connection with someone or a deepening of existing bonds. This card encourages you to make choices based on your heart's desires and values. It can also indicate a time of passionate commitment and emotional fulfillment.


Reversed

In a reversed position, The Lovers may indicate conflicts, disharmony, or challenges in relationships. You might be facing difficult decisions or experiencing conflicts of interest. This card encourages you to reevaluate your choices and prioritize communication and understanding. It may also signify a need to reconcile differences or reconsider a partnership that isn't aligned with your values. Be cautious when making important decisions, and strive for harmony in your relationships.
